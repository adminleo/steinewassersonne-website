## Project Description

For the Commercial Park South in Munich, we realized an extensive green roof installation. The project encompassed the greening of 2,400 m² of flat roof with a specially designed substrate layer and native sedum species.

## Challenges

The existing building's structural limitations required a lightweight solution. By using specialized substrate, the saturated weight was kept below 100 kg/m².

## Result

The completed roof surface not only provides improved stormwater retention but also creates valuable habitat for insects and birds. The building's energy costs were noticeably reduced through natural insulation.
