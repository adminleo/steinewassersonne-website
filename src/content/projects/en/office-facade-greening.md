## Project Description

For a modern office building in central Augsburg, a comprehensive facade greening concept was designed and implemented. The combination of ground-based climbing plants and wall-mounted planting modules creates a green envelope that architecturally enhances the building.

## Challenges

The southwest orientation of the facade required the selection of particularly heat-resistant plant species. Simultaneously, the irrigation system had to be integrated into the existing building technology.

## Result

The greened facade reduces surface temperatures in summer by up to 8°C and noticeably improves the microclimate in the surrounding street. A showcase project for urban building greening.
