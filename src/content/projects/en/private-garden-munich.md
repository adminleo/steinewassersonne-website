## Project Description

In Munich-Bogenhausen, a nature-inspired private garden was created that combines modern design elements with ecological responsibility. Natural stone walls made from regional limestone, a calming water feature, and carefully selected native perennials form the heart of the design.

## Concept

The garden is divided into several areas: a representative entrance, a sheltered terrace overlooking the water feature, and a naturalistic garden area with wildflower meadow and fruit trees.

## Result

A garden that serves as both a retreat and a nature experience — offering changing seasonal blooming aspects throughout the year.
